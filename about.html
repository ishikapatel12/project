About Us

HOME is a charity focused on creating a better world for homeless and elderly individuals. We aim to restore hope through creativity, music, art, and education. Our vision is a society where everyone is valued and supported.

Our Vision

A world where no one feels abandoned, and everyone can contribute meaningfully to society, regardless of their circumstances.
